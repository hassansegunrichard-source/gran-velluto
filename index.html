La Nostra Storia
I
In Italian tradition,
the table is sacred.
It is where families—
are built,
love is declared,
and the day finally exhales.
Gran Velluto was born in 1997 in the Brera district of Milano. Founded by Chef Emilio Conti using his grandmother's recipes, the kitchen has never chased trends — only truth. Today, his daughter Chef Giulia Conti leads the kitchen with the same devotion. Every dish is an act of memory. Every evening is an invitation.
